Strategy

Strategic Planning

is a process of looking into the future and identifying trends and issues against which to align organizational priorities of the Department or Office

\

Pillars of personal strategic planning



Diagnosis

It is a way to know the human reality through two basic questions: Who am I ? And where am I now ?

Targeting

is to identify dreams in aspects of life andto transfer these dreams from the area of imagination to the area of realism by presenting them to the conditions of the strategic goal.

Implementation

Begins with the first step and ends with the realization of the vision at the end of the plan.

Evaluation

It is a process that seeks to familiarize the individual with how to perform his plan, and sometimes it is to make a plan to improve and develop performance during implementation.
